Why Satsang Katha ?

Satsang Katha is an alternative, free, open-source YouTube like application for Android. It allows you to browse YouTube channels which are published comfortably without the need for Google/YouTube account and hence this app only offers read-only access to YouTube.


Here are some reasons why YOU should be using Satsang Katha:

Innovative Design

Contains novelty features such as most popular videos, video and thumbnail downloader, video reload, video rating on thumbnail... and more to come!

No ads

YouTube ads are now a thing of the past.

Google Apps Free

Satsang Katha is not dependent on the GAPPS/Google Apps (i.e. YouTube official app).
